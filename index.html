About Me

I found my passion for software engineering when I realized that it was the first professional skill I had spent my time learning that I could truly say that I genuinely enjoy. Since the very first I realized how much of the world around me was impacted by code, I was allured by the rapid development of technologies around me. Exploring new tools and understanding how they can simplify our lives inspires me every day. One of my greatest strengths is my background with computers, which I have spent much of my time on since early high-scool. This experience allowed me to bypass the usual learning curve, enabling me to focus more on development itself. One thing I am proud is that first step of teaching myself the fundamentals of Python in a short span, which speaks to my ability to learn quickly, both independently and with guidance. In the workplace, I bring strengths like teamwork, thoroughness, and positivity. I appreciate these qualities in myself and others. My favorite part of the development process is debugging; I find joy in solving problems and unraveling challenges. My dream job would allow me to concentrate on a specific task, giving me the space to achieve mastery. When I’m not coding, you can find me longboarding, enjoying the thrill of movement and the outdoors. As I continue my journey in software engineering, I’m excited to see where this passion will take me.
